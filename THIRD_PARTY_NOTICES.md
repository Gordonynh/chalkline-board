# Third Party Notices

## perfect-freehand

Pen stroke outline generation uses perfect-freehand.

- Project: https://github.com/steveruizok/perfect-freehand
- Package: perfect-freehand
- License: MIT

The library generates pressure-sensitive freehand stroke outlines with thinning, smoothing, streamline, and start/end taper options.

## polygon-clipping

Self-crossing pen outlines are flattened with polygon-clipping before rendering.

- Project: https://github.com/mfogel/polygon-clipping
- Package: polygon-clipping
- License: MIT
